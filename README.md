# Jacked — Community & Feedback

This is the public home for **Jacked** — an AI personal trainer that gets you stronger.

You chat with your coach. They remember everything, apply progressive overload automatically, and tell you exactly what to lift next session. No spreadsheets. No guesswork. Just show up and train.
